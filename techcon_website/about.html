Our History: From Startup to Global Summit

TechCon was first launched in 2010 as a small, regional meetup focused on open-source technologies. Driven by a passionate community of early adopters and disruptors, it quickly grew from a single-day event into a multi-track, international conference. Our initial focus was on fostering collaboration and providing a platform for unconventional ideas.
